draft a story about a time you had to break a big, confusing problem into smaller pieces to make progress on it.  

Situation : My BMW suddenly did not want to start and went into limp mode - a typical BMW problem 

Task : I need to figure out what is causing the bmw to go into limp mode and not allowing the car to start 

Action : i broke down the steps, pretty much making an algorithm or a flow chart on the steps needed for the car to take to start and i traced it. realised it was a bad fuel pump. 

Result: The car started immediately and has run without a single issue since — a repair that cost a fraction of what the dealership initially quoted for a full diagnostic, since I'd already isolated the exact part myself.
