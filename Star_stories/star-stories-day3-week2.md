# Situation 
I had a friend phone me once with an issue with his car. He is not a technical person and did not understand the logic behind how to diagnose if it was a bad alternator or a bad bbattery in the car. 
# Task 
I  had to break it down to him in non technical terms so he can isolate wat the issue was. 
# Action 
I explained it like his phone, if the battery was the issue then the phone wouldnt be able to turn on, as in no lights or no form of life is showing in the car and if the alternator was the issue then its like charging the phone, if the charger is the issue no matter how many times you charge the phone it still wont hold a charge 
# Result 

he started teh car with a jump cable and let it run for 20 minutes and then he killed the engine, when he turned off the car, and started it again the car started with no issue.
